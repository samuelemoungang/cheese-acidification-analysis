# Evaluation of Acidification Profiles in Cheese Making

## Introduction
This project investigates the influence of acidification profiles on the qualitative attributes of cheese. The acidification process, driven by starter cultures, is a critical phase in cheese making that determines the final product's texture, flavor, and safety.

## Summary
The study focuses on monitoring the activity of **starter cultures** throughout the cheese-making process. By evaluating how these cultures drive the acidification, we can better understand their impact on the final quality attributes. 

Key components of the analysis include:
* **Objective:** To evaluate how acidification profiles influence final cheese quality.
* **Process:** Acidification mediated by starter cultures during production.
* **Data Collection:** Systematic pH measurements over time and assessment of final product quality attributes.
